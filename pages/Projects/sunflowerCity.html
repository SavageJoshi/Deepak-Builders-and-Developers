Sunflower City

Pathardi Circle, Nashik

An oasis of calm and peace, Sunflower City is the home of our dreams. Distinguished apartments, accessible retail outlets, and a central location: What more could you need? Intricately designed by our team, Sunflower City is a superb combination of 464 residential & 29 commercial units across 2, 3.5 and 4 BHK apartments and 33+ amenities. Boasting beautiful landscaping across G+14 floors, this masterpiece comes with a gracefully crafted connected terrace space that adds to its allure. Experience the epitome of community living with Sunflower City, where you'll find the best community to call your own.
