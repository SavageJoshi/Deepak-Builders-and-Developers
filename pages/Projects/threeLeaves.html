Three Leaves

Nivrutti Modkari Estate, Highstreet, Govind Nagar, Nashik

Embrace luxury living at its finest with Three Leaves. Our signature and a blockbuster project of Nashik, boasts of 50+ best-in-class amenities along with 384 residential & 256 commercial units. Situated in the upcoming hub of Govind Nagar, this residential as well as commercial masterpiece is located in the heart of the city yet offers a calm and peaceful environment. Offering 3, 4 and 5 BHK apartments alongside a dedicated commercial unit, this is a 32-floor of luxury living (across three podiums) and 11 floors of affluent working. Three Leaves personifies opulence and elegance.
